PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated December 30, 2025
This Privacy Notice for Flare576.com ("we," "us," or "our") describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your personal information when you use our services ("Services"), including when you:
  • Visit our website at https://flare576.com
  • Connect your gaming accounts (Steam, Epic Games, etc.) to MyLibrary
  • Use our game library aggregation services

What Information Do We Collect?

MyLibrary is designed with privacy as a core principle. We only collect the minimum information necessary to provide our service:
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure tokens provided by gaming platforms to verify your identity
  • External IDs: Unique identifiers assigned by gaming platforms (Steam ID, Epic Account ID, etc.)
  • Game Library Data: Information about games you own on connected platforms

What We DON'T Collect

If someone hacks into MyLibrary's database, all they'll get is a list of generic tokens and External IDs that Steam, Epic, etc. give us to represent you. No names. No emails. No gender, age, race, religion, pasta preference... Nada.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:
  • Authenticate you with gaming platforms
  • Aggregate and display your game library
  • Provide search functionality across your games
  • Maintain your connected platform accounts

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your tokens and external IDs. All sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties, except as required to authenticate with gaming platforms or as required by law.

Your Rights

You have the right to:
  • Access your connected accounts
  • Disconnect any gaming platform at any time
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data

DO WE MAKE UPDATES TO THIS NOTICE?

In Short: Yes, we will update this notice as necessary to stay compliant with relevant laws.
We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by an updated "Revised" date at the top of this Privacy Notice. If we make material changes to this Privacy Notice, we may notify you either by prominently posting a notice of such changes or by directly sending you a notification. We encourage you to review this Privacy Notice frequently to be informed of our latest practices.
